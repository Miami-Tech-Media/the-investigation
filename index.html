Reports Method Reading Key
Ongoing public inquiry

What actually holds up, when you check?

A working investigation into the origins of Christianity — the manuscripts, the councils, the doctrines, the man at the center of it — built one verified topic at a time. Not apologetics. Not debunking. An honest accounting of what the evidence supports, what's genuinely contested, and what's still unknown.

01

Every claim is checked against primary sources, manuscript evidence, and the actual scholarly record — not assumed from tradition or from skepticism.

02

Theological and critical-scholarly positions are presented side by side. Where they genuinely diverge, both get stated plainly.

03

Certainty is marked, not implied. A claim that's settled looks different here than one that's still argued.

Reading Key — what the markers mean
Well-evidenced / broad scholarly agreement
Genuinely contested among scholars
Unresolved / beyond what evidence can settle
